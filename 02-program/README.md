# 02 — Program

MountainShares as a community economic and governance program. Audience: community
members, prospective participants, partners, and funders who want the substance, not
just the overview.

These pages describe the program as reconciled in the current governing documents
(the v2.0/v3.x structural rewrites). They use the **counsel-aligned** framing only:
M$ is a stable $1-pegged spend unit; EMS is recognition whose buying power can expand
on a conditional, reserve-gated schedule; the DUNA, not H4H, is the money-services
licensee; and the securities treatment is under counsel review with no representation
either way.

| Page | What it covers |
|---|---|
| [`token-model.md`](token-model.md) | The two value layers (PMS/M$ and EMS), the appreciation schedule, the two reserve ratios, fees and allocation |
| [`governance-charter.md`](governance-charter.md) | How the Commons is governed — voting, thresholds, what the DAO cannot override, the founder-as-steward principle |
| [`terms-and-conditions.md`](terms-and-conditions.md) | Participant terms (pre–Phase 0 review draft): nature of the system, what it is not, responsibilities |
| [`phases/`](phases/) | The phased rollout (0–3), the reserve gates that unlock each phase, and the reversion cascade |

**Exact parameter values** (fee percentages, reserve bands, caps) live in the
governing *Program Rules – Parameter Tables* and *Phase Specifications*. Where this
folder states a number, it is illustrative of the model; the governing documents
control. Nothing here is a present appreciation right, an investment, or a guarantee.

# Provenance

This repository is the audited, forward-facing subset of a larger record.

- **Work / lab repo:** https://github.com/H4HWV2011/msjarvis-public-docs
  The full development record: the 42-chapter thesis, methodology (`discipline/`),
  red-team evaluations, theory, and the complete history. Messier by design — it is
  the notebook, not the storefront.

- **This repo (MountainShares Commons):** the verified, single-named, audience-organized
  presentation layer. Numbers here are pinned to `04-evidence/` and regenerated from
  audit scripts, never hand-maintained.

Where the two disagree, the live system (as captured in `04-evidence/`) is
authoritative, and the work repo is corrected to match — not the reverse.
